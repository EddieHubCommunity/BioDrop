## Testimonials - with Forms

Show off the great feedback you have received.

This guide helps you manage Testimonials you have received where you already have a Profile (created with JSON or through Forms).

## Add a Testimonial to your Profile

1. Go to the [BioDrop homepage](https://biodrop.io/)

2. Click on `Log in`

![BioDrop Homepage](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/BioDrop/assets/82668196/ce980ed7-ad4d-42c0-886c-447a2e2ab936)

3. You will be taken to your Profile's Statistics page

Click on `Testimonials`

![BioDrop Profile](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/BioDrop/assets/82668196/6ea75170-adf2-4e2d-a9b1-90b1cce2c98d)

4. This will take you to the Testimonials page

All testimonials you have received will appear on this page

You can decide whether you want a particular testimonial to appear on your Profile by turning on the toggle

![BioDrop Add Testimonial page](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/BioDrop/assets/82668196/301920f0-c9b5-403a-80db-d1eaccf190b7)

5. Your milestone will then be shown in your Profile on your custom URL (BioDrop.io/eddiejaoude)

Note: if you wish to give a testimonial to another user then refer to the Docs for [Testimonials - with JSON](/docs/how-to-guides/testimonials-json)
