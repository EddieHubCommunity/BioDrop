## Editing - changing and adding to your Profile through Forms

### Edit your Profile

You can make changes and add to your existing Profile (whether you created this with JSON or through Forms) by completing a Form on the BioDrop app.

1. Go to the [BioDrop homepage](https://biodrop.io/)

2. Click on `Log in`

![BioDrop Homepage](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/BioDrop/assets/82668196/ce980ed7-ad4d-42c0-886c-447a2e2ab936)

3. You will be taken to your Profile's Statistics page

Click on `Profile`

![BioDrop Statistics page](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/BioDrop/assets/82668196/a724085f-0005-4327-966b-427aa61ef05c)

4. The Form will be populated with the information that you currently have in your Profile

You can change any of the form fields except `username` as this will always show your GitHub username

![BioDrop Forms page](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/BioDrop/assets/82668196/86119a20-412a-449a-acba-87df2510ad25)

5. Maximum 32 characters allowed in each tag , In case you exceed the characters than you can not separate or save the tag by comma.

Note: as you complete these fields the information will appear on the right hand side, which will allow you to preview how your Profile will look.

6. Click `Save`

7. Your changed Profile will then be available at your custom URL (BioDrop.io/eddiejaoude)
