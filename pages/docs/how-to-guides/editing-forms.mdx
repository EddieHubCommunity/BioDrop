## Editing - changing and adding to your Profile through Forms

### Edit your Profile

You can make changes and add to your existing Profile (whether you created this with JSON or through Forms) by completing a Form on the LinkFree app.

1. Go to the [LinkFree homepage](https://linkfree.io/)

2. Click on `Log in`

![LinkFree Homepage](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/LinkFree/assets/82668196/ce980ed7-ad4d-42c0-886c-447a2e2ab936)

3. You will be taken to your Profile's Statistics page

Click on `Profile`

![LinkFree Statistics page](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/LinkFree/assets/82668196/a724085f-0005-4327-966b-427aa61ef05c)

4. The Form will be populated with the information that you currently have in your Profile

You can change any of the form fields except `username` as this will always show your GitHub username

![LinkFree Forms page](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/LinkFree/assets/82668196/86119a20-412a-449a-acba-87df2510ad25)

Note: as you complete these fields the information will appear on the right hand side, which will allow you to preview how your Profile will look.

5. Click `Save`

6. Your changed Profile will be then be available on your custom URL (linkfree.io/eddiejaoude)
