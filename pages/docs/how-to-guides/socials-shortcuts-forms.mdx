## Social shortcuts - with Forms

Add a shortcut to your favourite social media accounts at the top of your Profile.

_Note: this must be one of your URLs links and will use the same icon from your link_

![Example of BioDrop Social Shortcut icons](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/BioDrop/assets/82668196/ba099bf6-d61a-4ee2-b8d5-09b2172f0b66)

### Add Social shortcuts

1. When adding or editing a link, for this to appear at the top of your Profile turn on the toggle at the bottom of the Form:

![BioDrop Links Form](https://github.com/EddieHubCommunity/BioDrop/assets/82668196/5cc517d7-1ad0-4d19-bb3c-b02301c2d803)

2. Click Save

3. The Social Shortcut will then be shown in your Profile on your custom URL (BioDrop.io/eddiejaoude)

If you have not yet added links to your Profile or you want to edit an existing link, take a look at the [Links - with Forms](/docs/how-to-guides/links-forms)
